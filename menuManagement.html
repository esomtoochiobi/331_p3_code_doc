menuManagement module¶

menuManagement.add_menu_item()¶

Adds a new menu item to the database along with its ingredients.

Param:JSON object containing details of the new menu item and its ingredients.
Returns:JSON response indicating whether the operation was successful or not.
menuManagement.archive_item_route(menu_id)¶

Archives a menu item.

Parameters:menu_id (int) – The ID of the menu item to archive.
Returns:Redirects to the menu management page with a flash message indicating success or failure.
menuManagement.edit_item_route(item_id)¶

Retrieves details of a menu item for editing.

Parameters:item_id (int) – The ID of the menu item to edit.
Returns:JSON object containing details of the menu item for editing or JSON response indicating success or failure of the update operation.
menuManagement.show_menuManagement()¶

Renders the menu management page.

Returns:HTML template for the menu management page populated with menu items and inventory data.
menuManagement.unarchive_item_route(menu_id)¶

Unarchives a menu item.

Parameters:menu_id (int) – The ID of the menu item to unarchive.
Returns:Redirects to the menu management page with a flash message indicating success or failure.
